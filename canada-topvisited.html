About this view

This page answers a simple question: of the websites Canadians actually visit most, how many already protect the connection against a future quantum computer? It is the same scan as the main monitor, run over a most-visited-by-Canadians list instead of the Canadian-institutions list. Most sites that pass do so because of their CDN, not their own servers - the PQC from column shows which.

Where the list comes from: the site list follows Similarweb's published Top Websites in Canada ranking, which orders sites by Canadian traffic and is refreshed monthly. Adult sites are excluded (they sit in the ranking but aren't relevant to a readiness report); everything else is kept as ranked, so the sample is a published, repeatable definition of "most visited by Canadians" rather than a hand-picked list. data/sites-ca-toplist.csv is that list in scan order.

This table still shows the 2026-07-16 scan of the previous, hand-picked list. The first scan of the sourced Similarweb list replaces it on the next scan day.
